# Tastes in Japanese

## Basic Tastes

| Hiragana | Kanji | Romaji | English | Type        |
|----------|-------|--------|---------|-------------|
| あまい      | 甘い    | amai   | sweet   | い-adjective |
| からい      | 辛い    | karai  | spicy   | い-adjective |
