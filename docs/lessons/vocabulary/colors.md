### Color Symbolism in Japan

- **Red (èµ¤)**: Associated with energy, passion, and celebration
- **White (ç™½)**: Symbol of purity, cleanliness, and death (funerals)
- **Black (é»’)**: Represents formality, elegance, and sometimes death
- **Blue (é’)**: Associated with calmness, nature, and the sea
- **Green (ç·‘)**: Represents nature, growth, and freshness
